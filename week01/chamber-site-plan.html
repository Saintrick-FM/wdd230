Site Purpose

Brazzaville Chamber of Commerce is a dedicated online platform designed to foster local business growth and community engagement in Brazzaville. This website will serve as a central hub for:

  • Providing local businesses with a platform to connect, collaborate, and promote their services to the community.
  • Offering the community information on local events, services, and opportunities through a dedicated, organized online resource.

Scenarios

  • How can I join the Chamber of Commerce? - A prospective business owner wanting to become a member can find membership options, benefits, and sign-up instructions.
  • What businesses are currently part of the Chamber? - A resident or visitor can view a directory of all businesses and organizations that are members.
  • What events are happening in Brazzaville? - Community members looking for networking events, local fairs, or Chamber-hosted activities can find event information and schedules.

Explanations for Each Section:

  • Home - The home page would focus on essential community info, weather updates, and highlight businesses and events.
  • Discover - An introduction to the area, using images and demographics.
  • Directory - A list of local businesses.
  • Join - Information and a form for potential members, covering membership benefits and levels.

Color Schema

Primary colors: for backgrounds and callouts.

Secondary colors: for headings and buttons.

Neutral colors: for text and content areas.

Typography

Header Font: Roboto - used for all headings.

Body Font: Merriweather - used for all body text.
